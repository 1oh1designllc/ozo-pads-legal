Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

OZO Pads ("the app", "we", "our") is developed and operated by 1Oh1 Design LLC dba ONE ZERO ONE ("the Company"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use OZO Pads on your iPhone or iPad.

We built OZO Pads for boxing coaches. We take your privacy seriously and have designed the app to collect as little data as possible.

1. Information We Collect

Data you create in the app. OZO Pads stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Fighter profiles (names, stance, weight class, skill level, fight record, coaching notes)
  • Combinations (names, punch sequences, difficulty ratings, video attachments, notes)
  • Session plans (names, combo selections, rep and rest settings)
  • Training log entries (session history, coach notes, duration)

All of this data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. It is not transmitted to our servers or any third party, except as described in Section 3 (AI Features).

Data we do not collect. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data from your contacts, camera roll, or other apps

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All fighter profiles, combos, session plans, and training logs are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device except when you choose to export it manually using the Export Backup feature in the app.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted. We recommend using the Export Backup feature regularly to keep a copy of your data.

3. AI Features (OZO Pads Pro)

OZO Pads Pro includes an AI combo variation feature powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you use this feature, the combination name and punch sequence you have selected are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate variation suggestions.

We do not send fighter names, personal information, coaching notes, or any other identifying data to Anthropic. Only the technical punch sequence data is transmitted.

Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

4. Subscription and Payments (OZO Pads Pro)

OZO Pads Pro subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.

Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat, which processes purchase verification on our behalf. RevenueCat may collect a device identifier for the purpose of verifying your subscription status. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

To manage or cancel your subscription, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone.

5. Camera and Photo Library

OZO Pads Pro allows you to attach demonstration videos to combinations. The app requests access to your camera and photo library only when you choose to record or attach a video. This media is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

6. Children's Privacy

OZO Pads is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of OZO Pads after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
